Groundnut Soup

Description

Groundnut (peanut) soup is a Ghanian local dish.

It's mainly made up with groundnut paste, some few vegetables and meat or if you like fish.

Ingredients

  • Groundnut paste
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes or tomato paste
  • meat or fish or both if you like.
  • Garlic
  • Peper
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Wash the meat and fish to make sure its clean and put them in the pan.
  2. Blend the onion, peper, ginger and garlic and add it to the meat and fish.
  3. Put the soup pan on fire, stir the meat and the blended vegetables to make sure it is well steamed.
  4. While the meat is on fire, dilute the groundnut paste with water.
  5. After some few minutes, add the diluted groundnut paste and the tomatoes.
  6. Again, after some few minutes take out the tomatoes, blend it and add it back to the soup.
  7. Add some salt.
